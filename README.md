## üõ†Ô∏è Features & Filters

The application supports the following image manipulations:

1.  **Grayscale Conversion:** Converts color images to black and white shades.
2.  **Black and White:** Converts image to strictly black and white pixels.
3.  **Invert Image:** Creates a negative of the image.
4.  **Merge Images:** Blends two images together.
5.  **Flip Image:** Flips the image horizontally or vertically.
6.  **Rotate Image:** Rotates the image by 90, 180, or 270 degrees.
7.  **Darken and Lighten:** Adjusts the brightness of the image.
8.  **Crop Image:** Cuts a specific rectangular area from the image.
9.  **Frame Image:** Adds a decorative frame to the image borders.
10. **Detect Image Edges:** Highlights the edges/boundaries within the image.
11. **Resize Image:** Scales the image dimensions up or down.
12. **Blur Image:** Applies a smoothing effect to reduce detail.
13. **Sunlight Filter:** Adds a warm, sunny tint to the image.
14. **Purple Filter:** Applies a purple color cast.
15. **Infrared Filter:** Simulates infrared photography visuals.
